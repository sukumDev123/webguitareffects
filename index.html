Testing a guitar converters

A guitar converters can make effects sound and delay sound
Firsting test time
This test can do

  • Delay sound of guitar and change delay time.
  • Creating sound on new wave to cover a old wave will can born new wave and can convert sound.
How to :
  • Step one : You must select file sound from your drive.
  • Step two : Clicking to play button, if you don't want to effects and delay are play, The sound will play original a guitar sound.
    If you want to effects or delay you must to choose checkbox "Open effects" for open effects and you can select type of sound . Type one is Highpass and two is Lowpass.
    if you want to delay sound you must select "Open Delay" to open delay and you can change delay time with range for change delay time is you need.
